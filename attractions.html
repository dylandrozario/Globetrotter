Top Attractions

Millennium Park & The Bean

Start at Millennium Park, home to Cloud Gate (aka "The Bean"). Grab that iconic mirror selfie, then explore the gardens, fountains, and free outdoor concerts at the Pritzker Pavilion.

Willis Tower Skydeck

Ride to the 103rd floor and step onto The Ledge — a glass box hanging over the city. It's a little scary, totally worth it, and the views of Chicago go on forever.

Navy Pier

Hit Navy Pier for the Centennial Wheel, lakefront views, street food, and boat tours. It's the perfect spot to hang out with friends as the sun sets over Lake Michigan.
