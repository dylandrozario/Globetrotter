Discover Chicago

Your first-timer's guide to the city's best eats, views, and good times.

Best Time to Visit

Late spring through early fall is the sweet spot — warm lakefront days, outdoor festivals, and patios everywhere. Summer is liveliest, while September brings smaller crowds and great weather.

Getting Around

Skip the car. The "L" train and buses get you almost anywhere for cheap, downtown is super walkable, and Divvy bikes are perfect for cruising the lakefront on a nice day.

Good to Know

Explore beyond downtown — neighborhoods like Wicker Park, Pilsen, and Lincoln Park each have their own vibe. Carry a card (most spots are cashless) and always dress in layers; the lake breeze is real.
