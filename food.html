Food Guide

Lou Malnati's Pizzeria

439 N Wells St, Chicago, IL 60654

The go-to for classic Chicago deep-dish. Buttery crust, gooey cheese, and chunky tomato sauce — this is the pizza you came to Chicago for.

Portillo's

100 W Ontario St, Chicago, IL 60654

Grab a Chicago-style hot dog (no ketchup!) and an Italian beef. Portillo's is a local institution and the perfect quick, tasty stop between sights.

Garrett Popcorn Shops

625 N Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60611

Try the famous "Garrett Mix" — caramel and cheese popcorn together. It sounds weird, tastes amazing, and makes the perfect snack while you explore the Mag Mile.
